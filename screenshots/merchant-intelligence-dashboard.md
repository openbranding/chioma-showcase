# Merchant Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

The Merchant Intelligence Dashboard provides a unified view of business readiness, operational quality, customer experience health, and knowledge effectiveness.

Rather than simply managing customer conversations, the dashboard continuously analyzes business operations and highlights areas that require attention.

---

## Strategic Purpose

The dashboard helps merchants understand:

- What customers need
- What information is missing
- What risks currently exist
- What improvements should happen next
- How business readiness changes over time

---

## Core Intelligence Areas

### Knowledge Intelligence

Provides visibility into:

- Knowledge coverage
- Missing information
- FAQ quality
- Product information quality
- Policy completeness

---

### Customer Experience Intelligence

Provides visibility into:

- Escalation trends
- Customer friction indicators
- Repeated customer questions
- Service quality concerns
- Experience risk factors

---

### Operational Intelligence

Provides visibility into:

- Process weaknesses
- Readiness gaps
- Operational risks
- Workflow maturity
- Business quality indicators

---

### Commerce Intelligence

Provides visibility into:

- Product readiness
- Catalog quality
- Ordering experience quality
- Pricing completeness
- Commerce risk indicators

## Long-Term Objective

The Merchant Intelligence Dashboard is designed to become the central operating environment through which merchants understand their business.

The objective is not merely to answer customer questions.

The objective is to continuously identify:

- Business weaknesses
- Customer experience risks
- Missing knowledge
- Operational opportunities
- Improvement priorities

before they negatively impact customers.
