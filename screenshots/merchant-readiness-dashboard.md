# Merchant Readiness Dashboard

## Overview

The Merchant Readiness Dashboard provides visibility into the operational health of a business.

Rather than focusing only on customer conversations, the dashboard evaluates whether the business is prepared to consistently deliver accurate customer experiences.

---

# Primary Objectives

The dashboard helps merchants:

- Measure operational readiness
- Detect business weaknesses
- Monitor knowledge completeness
- Identify customer experience risks
- Prioritize improvements

---

# Readiness Categories

## Knowledge Readiness

Measures:

- FAQ completeness
- Product coverage
- Policy coverage
- Information quality

---

## Customer Support Readiness

Measures:

- Escalation preparedness
- Response quality
- Human handoff readiness
- Support process maturity

---

## Commerce Readiness

Measures:

- Product availability visibility
- Pricing completeness
- Ordering process quality
- Catalog accuracy

---

## Operational Readiness

Measures:

- Business process coverage
- Internal procedure documentation
- Workflow maturity
- Risk exposure

---

# Dashboard Components

## Readiness Score

Displays:

- Overall readiness score
- Category scores
- Historical trends

---

## Knowledge Gaps

Displays:

- Missing FAQs
- Missing policies
- Missing product information
- Incomplete business knowledge

---

## Risk Indicators

Displays:

- Customer experience risks
- Operational risks
- Knowledge risks
- Support risks

---

## Improvement Opportunities

Displays:

- Recommended actions
- Priority improvements
- Readiness gains
- Suggested next steps

# Strategic Purpose

The Readiness Dashboard helps merchants understand:

- What is working
- What is missing
- What needs improvement
- What risks exist
- What actions should happen next

The objective is to make operational readiness visible and measurable.
