# Current Chioma Interface

## Overview

The screenshots below represent the current production-ready version of Chioma.

Chioma is currently undergoing a major Merchant Intelligence upgrade.

Some interfaces shown here may evolve as the new Merchant Intelligence, Readiness Scoring, and Knowledge Governance capabilities are introduced.

## Existing Capabilities

- WhatsApp Customer Engagement
- AI-Powered Customer Support
- Merchant Knowledge Base
- FAQ Management
- Customer Conversations
- Merchant Dashboard
- Analytics & Reporting

## Screenshots

### Merchant Workspace Dashboard

Current production dashboard used by merchants to manage WhatsApp engagement, monitor readiness, review business knowledge, and maintain customer support operations.

Key capabilities visible:

- WhatsApp connectivity management
- Merchant onboarding status
- Knowledge readiness monitoring
- Business knowledge management
- Quality audit access
- Guided setup workflows
- Customer conversation access
