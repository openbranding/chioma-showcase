# Merchant Intelligence

## Overview

Merchant Intelligence is the operational intelligence layer of Chioma.

Instead of functioning as a simple chatbot, Chioma continuously evaluates a merchant's business readiness and identifies information required for accurate customer engagement.

The system helps merchants improve their operational knowledge, customer support readiness, and commerce workflows.

---

## Objectives

* Identify missing business information
* Detect weak knowledge areas
* Improve customer response quality
* Increase operational readiness
* Reduce support bottlenecks
* Improve commerce conversion outcomes

---

## Core Capabilities

### Knowledge Gap Detection

Chioma identifies missing information required to answer customer questions effectively.

### Readiness Assessment

The platform evaluates how prepared a merchant is to support customers across different operational areas.

### Guided Knowledge Collection

Merchants are guided through structured questions that improve business knowledge coverage.

### Operational Recommendations

Chioma proposes improvements that help merchants strengthen customer engagement and business operations.

---

## Benefits

* Better customer experiences
* Faster onboarding
* Improved support quality
* Reduced information gaps
* Increased merchant confidence
* Stronger operational consistency
