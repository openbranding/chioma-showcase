# Chioma Conversation OS

## Overview

Chioma uses a Conversation Operating System (Conversation OS) to coordinate customer engagement workflows.

The Conversation OS provides a structured approach for:

- Customer conversations
- Workflow execution
- Business logic orchestration
- Merchant knowledge retrieval
- AI-assisted responses

---

## Core Objectives

1. Consistent customer engagement

2. Controlled workflow execution

3. Reliable merchant knowledge usage

4. Human-governed automation

5. Scalable architecture

---

## Major Components

### Conversation State

Tracks conversation progress and context.

### Workflow Orchestrator

Coordinates business workflow execution.

### Action Registry

Maintains available actions and capabilities.

### Observer System

Provides monitoring and event visibility.

### Reply Engine

Generates customer-facing responses.

## Benefits

- Improved consistency
- Better customer support
- Structured automation
- Easier maintenance
- Scalable growth
