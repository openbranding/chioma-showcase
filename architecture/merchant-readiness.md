# Merchant Readiness

## Overview

Merchant Readiness measures how prepared a business is to support customers, answer questions, process requests, and operate effectively.

Chioma continuously evaluates readiness across multiple operational dimensions.

---

## Readiness Areas

### Business Information

Availability of company information and policies.

### Product Knowledge

Availability of product and service information.

### Customer Support

Ability to answer customer questions accurately.

### Commerce Operations

Ability to process customer requests and transactions.

### Escalation Handling

Availability of fallback and handoff procedures.

---

## Readiness Assessment

Chioma identifies:

* Missing information
* Weak knowledge areas
* Incomplete workflows
* Unsupported customer journeys

---

## Outcomes

The readiness system helps merchants:

* Improve customer experience
* Reduce operational gaps
* Increase consistency
* Improve support quality
* Strengthen business operations
