# Chioma Platform Map

## Overview

Chioma is evolving from a WhatsApp Commerce and Customer Support platform into a Merchant Intelligence Platform.

The platform combines customer engagement, business knowledge management, operational readiness assessment, governance, and continuous business improvement.

This document provides a high-level map of the platform.

---

# Platform Layers

## Layer 1

### Customer Engagement

Responsible for customer-facing communication.

Includes:

- WhatsApp conversations
- Human handover
- Escalations
- Broadcast campaigns
- Customer interaction history

Primary objective:

Help merchants communicate with customers effectively.

---

## Layer 2

### Knowledge Operations

Responsible for managing business knowledge.

Includes:

- FAQs
- Products
- Policies
- Procedures
- Business information

Primary objective:

Ensure accurate and reliable customer responses.

---

## Layer 3

### Merchant Intelligence

Responsible for understanding business readiness.

Includes:

- Readiness assessments
- Knowledge gap detection
- Operational risk detection
- Customer experience risk analysis
- Business quality monitoring

Primary objective:

Identify weaknesses before they affect customers.

---

## Layer 4

### Readiness Management

Responsible for measuring operational quality.

Includes:

- Readiness scoring
- Department scoring
- Knowledge readiness
- Support readiness
- Commerce readiness

Primary objective:

Make business readiness measurable.

---

## Layer 5

### Knowledge Governance

Responsible for controlling business knowledge.

Includes:

- Draft workflows
- Approval workflows
- Knowledge versioning
- Quality validation
- Governance controls

Primary objective:

Ensure business knowledge remains trustworthy.

---

## Layer 6

### Continuous Intelligence

Responsible for ongoing business improvement.

Includes:

- Continuous monitoring
- Trend analysis
- Improvement recommendations
- Risk forecasting
- Operational intelligence

Primary objective:

Continuously improve business performance.

# Strategic Direction

Traditional customer support systems answer questions.

Chioma seeks to improve the operational knowledge behind those answers.

The platform evolves from:

Customer Support Platform

↓

Commerce Platform

↓

Knowledge Platform

↓

Merchant Intelligence Platform

↓

Business Readiness Platform

↓

Merchant Operating System

---

# Long-Term Goal

Help merchants continuously understand:

- What customers need
- What knowledge is missing
- What operational risks exist
- What improvements should happen next
- How readiness changes over time

Chioma becomes the operating environment through which businesses improve customer engagement, business knowledge, and operational readiness.
