# Merchant Intelligence Engine

## Overview

The Merchant Intelligence Engine is the operational intelligence layer of Chioma.

Instead of functioning as a simple chatbot, Chioma continuously evaluates business readiness, knowledge quality, customer support capability, commerce operations, and customer engagement effectiveness.

The engine helps merchants identify weaknesses, improve operational quality, and provide better customer experiences.

---

## Purpose

The Merchant Intelligence Engine exists to help businesses:

- Improve operational readiness
- Improve customer engagement quality
- Improve knowledge coverage
- Reduce support failures
- Detect business information gaps
- Improve customer satisfaction
- Increase operational consistency

---

## Core Responsibilities

### Readiness Evaluation

The engine continuously evaluates how prepared a business is to support customers.

Areas include:

- Business Information
- Product Knowledge
- Customer Support
- Commerce Operations
- Policy Coverage

---

### Knowledge Analysis

The engine evaluates the quality and completeness of merchant knowledge.

Examples:

- Missing FAQs
- Weak product descriptions
- Missing policies
- Incomplete operational guidance
- Outdated information

---

### Gap Detection

The engine identifies operational weaknesses and missing information.

Examples:

- Missing refund policy
- Missing delivery information
- Missing escalation path
- Incomplete support procedures
- Incomplete product information

---

### Improvement Recommendations

The engine proposes improvements that may help increase readiness and customer experience quality.

Examples:

- Suggested FAQ content
- Policy recommendations
- Product information improvements
- Customer support enhancements
- Operational workflow recommendations

---

### Conversation Intelligence

The engine learns from customer interactions.

It analyzes:

- Frequently asked questions
- Escalation patterns
- Unanswered questions
- Repeated customer issues
- Knowledge coverage weaknesses

---

### Commerce Intelligence

The engine evaluates operational commerce readiness.

Examples:

- Ordering workflows
- Product availability information
- Payment instructions
- Delivery information
- Fulfillment guidance

---

## Governance Integration

The Merchant Intelligence Engine does not directly modify merchant knowledge.

All proposed improvements must pass through Knowledge Governance and Merchant Approval workflows before becoming active.

---

```text
Customer Interaction

        ↓

```

---

## Business Value

The Merchant Intelligence Engine transforms Chioma from a customer-response system into a continuous business improvement platform.

The goal is not only to answer customer questions, but also to help merchants become better prepared, better informed, and more operationally effective over time.
