# Knowledge Governance

## Overview

Knowledge Governance ensures that merchant knowledge remains accurate, approved, and controlled.

Chioma never silently modifies business information. All proposed improvements require merchant review and approval.

---

## Governance Principles

### Merchant Ownership

Business knowledge always belongs to the merchant.

### Human Approval

Suggested changes remain in draft form until approved.

### Auditability

Changes can be reviewed and tracked.

### Transparency

Merchants can see what information is being proposed and why.

---

## Knowledge Lifecycle

1. Knowledge Gap Detected
2. Recommendation Generated
3. Draft Created
4. Merchant Review
5. Merchant Approval
6. Knowledge Published

---

## Benefits

* Improved trust
* Reduced misinformation
* Stronger operational control
* Clear accountability
* Safer AI-assisted knowledge improvement
