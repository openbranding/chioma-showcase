# Merchant Onboarding Workflow

## Objective

Guide a merchant from registration to operational readiness.

---

## Workflow Steps

### Step 1

Merchant registration.

### Step 2

Business profile setup.

### Step 3

Knowledge collection.

### Step 4

Policy configuration.

### Step 5

Workflow configuration.

### Step 6

Validation and review.

### Step 7

Activation.

---

## Outcome

Merchant becomes ready for customer engagement.
